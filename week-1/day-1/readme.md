1. What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer data, communication, sales, and services in one place.

2. Why companies use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it helps manage customers, track sales, improve communication, automate work, and increase business productivity.

3. Explain

 Account

An Account represents a company or organization with whom the business has a relationship.

Contact

A Contact is a person associated with an Account, such as a customer or employee.

Opportunity

An Opportunity represents a potential sales deal or business deal in progress.

4. Real-world mapping

Salesforce Object	Real-world Example

Account	Infosys company
Contact	HR manager of Infosys
Opportunity	Software project deal with Infosys
